HOW TO USE THIS SITE

An intuitive search engine for all Jeopardy lovers.

1. Use the Search feature to search for Jeopardy hints by date/time aired (either by day, week, or month), trivia category, level of difficulty, and the offset of results you would like shown. You can mix and match these criteria to refine your results as you want. If you want a random clue, just press the Random button!

2. Press the "Clear Search" button to clear your current search results and search again.

3. If you still have any questions, please use the "Contact" feature to ask me any questions and I will get back to you as soon as possible!

4. Enjoy :)
